The following blog will contain some key features in American University Websites and Irish University Websites.

## American Universities

- [University of Alaska](https://www.alaska.edu/alaska/)
- [University of Arkansas](https://uafs.edu/)
- [Athens State University](https://www.athens.edu/)
- [Arizona State University](https://asuonline.asu.edu/)

## Key features

- Large hero section with images and text on landing pages
- Use of red and blue color on most websites
- Big and Bold Font
- More animations and hover interactions

## Irish Universities

- [Dublin City University](https://www.dcu.ie/)
- [Maynooth University](https://www.maynoothuniversity.ie/)
- [University of Galway](https://www.universityofgalway.ie/?ref=logo#)
- [Trinity College Dublin](https://www.tcd.ie/)

## Key features

- Large hero section with images and text on landing pages same as American University websites
- Use of green and blue color mostly
- Elegant and classic fonts
- Very simple and minimalist hover interactions and almost no animations
